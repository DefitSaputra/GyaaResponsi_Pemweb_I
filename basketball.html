Basketball

About Basketball

Basketball is a dynamic and fast-paced team sport, played by two teams of five players each. The objective is to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop.

Basketball Rules

Games are typically played in four quarters of 12 minutes each. Players must dribble the ball while moving, and the game includes techniques like shooting, passing, and rebounding.


Famous Basketball Players

  • Michael Jordan
  • LeBron James
  • Kobe Bryant
  • Stephen Curry

Popular Basketball Leagues

The NBA (National Basketball Association) is the most famous basketball league globally, featuring some of the best talent and high-intensity games.
